====== Topics for Discussion ======

  * To add a new topic
      - Create a new page under the ''/topics'' heading by appending ''/my-new-topic-page'' to this page's URL in your address bar
      - Click Create New Page
      - Copy & paste the source from the [[http://wiki.csswg.org/topics/new-topic?do=edit|Template for New Topics]]
      - Fill it out
  * To find topics, use the tag clouds to filter and/or click table headings to sort.

==== Open Topics ====
