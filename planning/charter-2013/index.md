<h2 id="charter-2013-2015-planning">Charter 2013-2015 planning</h2>
<p>
Editors, please, fill in for your drafts what level you think the drafts will reach within the next two years, REC, CR, WD, NOTE or RSCND (Rescinded REC), and by when.
</p>

<p>
Once the WG is happy with the list, all the drafts that reach a level different from their current one will become <em>deliverables</em> in the charter and the others will be mentioned as items that are <em>in scope.</em> (Mentioning them explicitly helps to reduce possible patent problems.)
</p>

<p>
Use the Notes column for notes to ourselves. They won&#039;t be in the charter.
</p>
