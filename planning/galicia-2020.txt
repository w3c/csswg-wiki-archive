   * Location: [[https://goo.gl/maps/34b8ARCndsT2|Igalia, A Coruña, Galicia, Spain]]

     * Date & time: 20:30 on Wednesday 22nd Janaury

     * Date & time: 19:30-21:00 on Thursday 23rd January
