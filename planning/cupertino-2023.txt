====== Cupertino F2F July 2023 ======

   * Host: Apple
   * Location: One Apple Park Way, Cupertino CA 95014, USA
       * Visitor entrance is at [[https://goo.gl/maps/cGQYeU23PkEGfnpn7|Tantau Reception Center]], 10503 North Tantau Avenue, Cupertino, CA 95014.  This is adjacent to the intersection of North Tantau Avenue and Pruneridge Avenue.
       * If you're driving there, the visitor parking is in an underground garage whose entrance is [[https://goo.gl/maps/371ED4eugJoaGooy5|at this point]], adjacent to the Apple Park Visitor Center, 10600 North Tantau Avenue, Cupertino, CA 95014.  This is just across North Tantau Avenue from the visitor entrance.
   * Dates: Tuesday 18 July - Friday 21 July 2023
   * Airports:
       * SJC - San Jose Airport (16km drive, **closest**, be careful to use correct airport code)
       * SFO - San Francisco Airport (54km drive, **major hub**)
       * OAK - Oakland Airport (69km drive)
   * Schedule: possibly starting at 8am to better accommodate Europe (and to avoid traffic)?
