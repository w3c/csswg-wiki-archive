TPAC 2012

CSS will meet during TPAC 2012, which runs from Monday 29 October through Friday 2 November, in Lyon, France. The CSS working group will be meeting on Sunday, Monday, and Tuesday, October 28-30.

Logistics: TPAC 2012 (including registration)

Meeting Minutes

Sunday, 28 October:

  • Sunday AM I, Style Attributes, Writing Modes, Text, Text Decoration
  • Sunday AM II, Spec Shortnames, Prioritization
  • Sunday AM III, Conditional Rules, Sizing
  • Sunday PM I, Collisions, Exclusions, Line Layout
  • Sunday PM II, Paged Media, Box, Fragmentation, Overflow Regions

Monday, 29 October:

  • Monday AM I, HTMLWG/CSSWG Coordination
  • Monday AM II, Regions, Multicol
  • Monday PM I, Masking, Compositing
  • Monday PM II, Animations, Transforms

Tuesday, 30 October:

  • Tuesday AM I, Abstract Directions, Transforms, Transitions
  • Tuesday AM II, text-overflow, case-sensitivity, Grid Layout
  • Tuesday PM I, AltSS, Cascade, HTML5, Conditional Rules
  • Tuesday PM II, Display Models, Animations
