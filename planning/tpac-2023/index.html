CSSWG meeting at TPAC 2023

TPAC 2023 is scheduled for 11-15 September 2023 in Sevilla, Spain, with options for both in-person and remote participation.

  • CSSWG F2F: Thursday-Friday, 14-15 September, 2023. Meeting room available 9:30-18:30 CEST. (see overall schedule)
  • HTML/WHATWG-CSSWG Joint Meeting: 14:30-16:30 CEST Thu 14 Sept.
  • A11y-CSSWG Joint Meeting: 17:00-18:00 CEST Fri 15 Sept.
  • i18n-CSSWG Joint Meeting 11:30-12:30 CEST Thu 14 Sept.
