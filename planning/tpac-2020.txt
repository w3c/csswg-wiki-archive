====== CSSWG 3nd Virtual F2F, at TPAC 2020 ======


[[https://www.w3.org/2020/10/TPAC/|TPAC Virtual Meeting]] is scheduled for **October 2020**

  * 12-16 October for [[https://www.w3.org/wiki/TPAC/2020/GroupMeetings|joint meetings with other groups]] (TBD groups include I18N, TTWG, APA, MathML?, SVGWG?)
  * 19-23 October for our own meetings; see [[https://www.w3.org/wiki/TPAC/2020/GroupMeetings|W3C-wide group meeting calendar]]
  * 26-30 October is [[https://www.w3.org/2020/10/TPAC/breakout-schedule.html|breakout week]]
