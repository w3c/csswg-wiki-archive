<h1 id="meeting-planning-pages">Meeting Planning Pages</h1>
<p>
This is the <abbr title="Cascading Style Sheets">CSS</abbr> Working Group&#039;s planning space.
</p>

<p>
The chairs will add most events to the <abbr title="World Wide Web Consortium">W3C</abbr> calendar, which should show up in <strong><a href="https://www.w3.org/users/myprofile/calendar/" title="https://www.w3.org/users/myprofile/calendar/" rel="noopener">your W3C calendar</a></strong> (see export options at the bottom) if you&#039;re a member of the <abbr title="Cascading Style Sheets">CSS</abbr> Working Group.  An unofficial <strong><a href="https://cal.csswg.org/public.php/csswg/calendar.ics" title="https://cal.csswg.org/public.php/csswg/calendar.ics" rel="noopener">CSSWG Meeting iCalendar Feed</a></strong> is also available.
</p>

<p>
All meetings use <abbr title="Internet Relay Chat">IRC</abbr> for live minuting and as a backchannel: the #css channel on <a href="https://www.w3.org/Project/IRC/" title="https://www.w3.org/Project/IRC/" rel="noopener">the W3C IRC server</a>.
