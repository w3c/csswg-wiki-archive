Seattle F2F July 2011

Topics for the July 2011 CSS Working Group Face-to-Face meeting in Seattle, WA.

  • When: Sunday 24 – Tuesday 26 (adjacent to SVG meeting with joint meeting on Tusday July 26)
  • Where: South Lake Union Mariott Courtyard, Room: Puget Sound
  • Registration: survey
  • Weather: °C / °F

Meeting Minutes

Sunday, 24 July

  • Sunday am - IVS, Flexbox, HTML5
  • Sunday pm - F2Fs, Selectors 4, CSS3 Text, Serialization, Testing

Monday, 25 July

  • Monday am and early pm (with all Monday IRC) - CSSOM, Regions, Floats/Exclusions
  • rest of Monday pm - Writing Modes, Gradients, Grid, Positioning

Tuesday, 26 July

  • Tuesday (joint with SVG) - paint servers, pointer-events
