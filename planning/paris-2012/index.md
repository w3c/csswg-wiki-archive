<h1 id="paris-f2f-february-2012">Paris F2F February 2012</h1>
<p>
Topics for the February 2012 <abbr title="Cascading Style Sheets">CSS</abbr> Working Group Face-to-Face meeting in Paris, France.
</p>

<p>
<strong>When:</strong> Monday 6 February – Wednesday 8 February
</p>

<p>
<strong>Where:</strong> <a href="http://maps.google.com/maps?q=23+avenue+d%27Italie,+paris&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;sll=46.75984,1.738281&amp;sspn=8.943171,23.269043&amp;vpsrc=0&amp;hnear=23+Avenue+d%27Italie,+75013+Paris,+%C3%8Ele-de-France&amp;t=m&amp;z=16" title="http://maps.google.com/maps?q=23+avenue+d%27Italie,+paris&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;sll=46.75984,1.738281&amp;sspn=8.943171,23.269043&amp;vpsrc=0&amp;hnear=23+Avenue+d%27Italie,+75013+Paris,+%C3%8Ele-de-France&amp;t=m&amp;z=16" rel="noopener">23 avenue d&#039;Italie</a> 75012 Paris
</p>

<p>
Room: Blue room Monday, Green room Tuesday and Wednesday, 5th floor
</p>

<p>
<strong>Registration:</strong> TBD (none? this link is broken: <a href="http://www.w3.org/2002/09/wbs/99999/csswg-f2f-paris-2012-01/" title="http://www.w3.org/2002/09/wbs/99999/csswg-f2f-paris-2012-01/" rel="noopener">http://www.w3.org/2002/09/wbs/99999/csswg-f2f-paris-2012-01/</a> )
</p>
